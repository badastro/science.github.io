About Camera Scouts

At Camp Pioneer, there is an elite team of scouts dedicated to capturing its memories. When a rookie named Brenden Stott joins the Camera Scouts, there is more than meets the lens.

The short film 'Camera Scouts' is David Quang Pham's pride and joy. At Camp Frontier on the borders of Michigan and Ohio, David Quang Pham taught the photography and astronomy merit badge in 2015 and 2016.

Cast: Brenden Stott, Patrick Casteels, Alex Marzari, Jonathan Perry, Evan Zobel, David Quang Pham

Director of Photography: Jonathan Perry

Music: Sidewatchers, Zoe Kissel

Location: Camp Frontier at Pioneer Scout Reservation, Pioneer, Ohio
