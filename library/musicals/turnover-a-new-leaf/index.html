Plot

Set in a world of personified plants, this coming-of-age musical follows a transplant cilantro who starts a salon in Dirty City to raise her son, Parsley. When the garden nation of Woodwook ratifies the Weed Out Act, she must earn her green card before she gets uprooted and loses custody of Parsley. However, she faces a new obstacle: her landlord Malus's nibling and Parsley's new pear friend named Pyrus Crabapple. Pyrus sends Parsley on an adventure filled with crop tops and fertilizer pops that gives him an awakening his mom was not rooting for.

Photosynthesis: The Motion Picture

Photosynthesis is the animated film and series adaptation that expands upon the plant-based stage musical Turnover: A New Leaf. It was a 2025 Creative Capital finalist. Visit PhotosynthesisFilm.com for the treatment and script.

Author

David Quang Pham is an award-winning Vietnamese-American songwriter and filmmaker based in Manhattan, known for creating musicals that personify scientific concepts to explore the human condition. His work has been produced or developed by Queer Theatre Kalamazoo, Milky Way Theatre (Off-Broadway), Musical Theatre Factory, Signature Theatre, with performances in Colorado, Georgia, Michigan, Pennsylvania, and Portugal. His artistic talents led to his Working Title Playwrights apprenticeship, Playwrights Foundation fellowship, Great Performances Fellowship Award, and 78th Tony Awards CBS appearance. They hold a B.S. in Astrophysics with honors and Minor in Theatre from Michigan State University.
